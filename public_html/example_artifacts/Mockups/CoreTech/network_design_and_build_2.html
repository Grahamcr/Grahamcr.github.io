The Network Design and Build team builds the networks that allow GE employees to access websites, share and print digital files, meet across continents using telepresence and attend webex meetings via cell phones. CoreTech designs, implements, and provide administrative support.


Networks link computers with everything from cables and telephone lines to radio waves and satellites. The components of a network are many, including cables, routers, switches, servers and workstations and all the parts of a Local Area Network (LAN) and Wide Area Network (WAN). CoreTech engineers apply their skills and knowledge of best practices to ensure that GE businesses have the right array of components and that they operate and are maintained.


CoreTech offers site surveys, design, deployment, testing and proactive monitoring and management services. The team offers “boots on the ground” to coordinate with component vendors. If necessary to set up service, the team will travel to your site.

Includes

WAN, Data Edge devices, LAN Switches, Wireless LAN & Controllers/Access Points, VPN / Encryption Devices, Proxy / IPS / Optimizer devices, WAAS.


Does Not Include:

• Onsite deployment (rack/stack/cabling/onsite testing or verification with users)

• Travel & Living costs

• Conducting on–site site survey
