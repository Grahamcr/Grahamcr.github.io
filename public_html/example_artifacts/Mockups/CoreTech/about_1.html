Product Strategy and Customer Engagement

"Working with our customers, partners and other GE Digital teams, this team determines the CoreTech product roadmap."

Team members engage directly with multiple constituencies, including internal customers and engineers, industry organizations and external customers-many of them with competing demands. They learn the main drivers behind requests, and from this understanding they work to develop a prioritized roadmap. They then work with the Product Life Cycle Management team to ready products for production. Once our products are in the market, the team trains our users so they understand how the products work and can develop new and creative ways to use them.

Chris Drumgoole

CoreTech Solutions Leader

“CoreTech is radically focused on providing solutions for GE businesses to enable our digital industrial future. We are building teams of full-stack engineers and leaders who know how to build and deploy world class services for applications to run on.


Historically, IT has organized around technologies, but today our teams must focus on outcomes—delivering applications and technology that scale across GE and our customer base. This will help deliver better results for GE, our customers and their customers.”

Product Life Cycle Management

"Working from the product roadmap, this team collaborates with architects and application developers in Engineering, Operations, Finance and other organizations in the supply chain to bring our products and services to life."

Team members are responsible for ensuring that our products and services get to market, serve the market well and leave the market when the time is right. They work closely with the Product Strategy and Customer Engagement team.

Engineering

"The engineers, architects and developers on this team design and build our offerings."

They assemble cloud and traditional applications, solutions, tools and services with an eye toward ensuring that applications are automated, self-service and can continuously operate (survivability). They build, test, and deliver solutions that the Operations team can then deploy at mass scale.

Client Services

"This team is responsible for elevating customer satisfaction by reinventing CoreTech support. It leads all customer-centric support initiatives."

Team members manage the customer support “touch-points” end to end. They empower regional infrastructure leaders to partner day-to-day with regional CIOs and business leaders to give our customers a single point of contact for CoreTech. They also ensure that regional IT aligns with GE Digital strategy. Together with Product Strategy and Customer Engagement, this team listens to and responds to customer needs (voice of customer).

Operations

"This team ensures that all of our solutions and services—such as core services, applications, Infrastructure as a Service (IaaS) and data centers—run to our standards."

Operating 24x7x365 across the globe, the members of this team are our front line of response and execution for planned and unplanned incidents.

Application Modernization

"This team unlocks business value by modernizing the application portfolio across GE."

Team members collaborate with CIOs, application teams and Risk to develop a systemic, disciplined approach to application infrastructure, determining what “good” looks like for GE. They develop a roadmap to ensure that all GE solutions run on modern, next-generation architecture, maximizing value for GE. While other CoreTech foundational teams develop and deliver solutions, this team establishes the application playbook for a state-of-the-art infrastructure that supports the application of the future.

Strategic Initiatives

"This team is the keystone of the CoreTech culture. It supports each of our teams with the necessary tools and processes to make us more agile, more aware and more efficient."

Team members work with CoreTech leaders to provide education, training, communications and organizational support for both CoreTech teams and customers, in partnership with GE human resources (HR) and training teams. They also team with HR to shepherd our hiring practices, ensuring we are putting the right talent to work in the right manner at the right location.
