Home

A/B Test Observation Takeways

• Split the "Bring GhostRed To You Page" into two different pages, one for internal and one for external

• Focus more on the association with GE Digital

• Add clarity around interviews/jobs with GE from both perspectives,

       • Participants (Do I have to want a job? Can I just for fun?) and

       • Managers (What the process like to hire from GhostRed?)

• Clarify if users are requesting an event or scheduling an event

• Improve the Navigation to be more inclusive of content within the site (more accurate mental model based on navigation options)

• Make sure "Below Fold" content is initially visible to ensure users know they can scroll down

• Include a blog and/or a way to stay up to date with GhostRed and upcoming events

• Include a way to integrate social media

• Add left anchor links to event details page

• "What is GhostRed" is the most frequently picked first click of A/B Test participants, second most frequent is find an event

• Show what participant is responsible for (what happens after I sign up for an event?)

• Show what the benefit of a GhostRed event is over just applying for a job on GE.com? Why go through all the trouble?

• Should interviews be a prize? Maybe not?

• Should have walk through as part of the demo

• Why schedule a GhostRed event over the alternative ways of attracting talent at GE?

• Form teams before an event begins? A forum-ish thing?

• User Account (Registered Events, Team, History, Updates, etc)

• Support Multiple Day Events

Proposal for Future Work

• Create a second iteration design which keeps the look and feel of Design A (Skull) but

       • Add exploitability/interaction opportunities to find out more information about what it is like to participate in GhostRed (What does it look like, what are the challenges, do I have to want a job?) or

       • Request to put on a GhostRed Event (what am I required to do, are participants screened, do I have to interview everyone, how is the recruitment team involved?). 

       • Second Iteration should also firmly depict our association with GE Digital, which might require further discussion within GhostRed to determine our ideal direction in this area. 

        • Finally the content also needs to address unfulfilled motivations of personas, ex. Why go to GhostRed if I could just apply to a job? Why run a GhostRed event over a job fair?
