Initial User Research

For the inital user research, Max and I paired to speak with Hiring Managers, Recruiters, Internal and External employees in the digital space.  We spoke with them about their job responsibilities and how they thought GhostRed could help them overcome some of the biggest challenges in their involvement with recruiting new talent.  With external employees, Max gauged what their perception was of GE as a place to work and what they are looking for in the ideal employer

First Iteration Designs

Two designs purposefully created to be polar opposites of one another to test not only the look and feel that our primary personas will best respond to, but how they will best understand visually what GhostRed is, does and means.  Design A focuses on what GhostRed is and tries to be very visually appealing while Design B is focused on the experience of participating or putting on a GhostRed event while being visually calm.

Second Iteration Design

Create a second iteration design which keeps the look and feel of Design A (Skull) but add exploitability/interaction opportunities to find out more information about what it is like to participate in GhostRed (What does it look like, what are the challenges, do I have to want a job?) or request to put on a GhostRed Event (what am I required to do, are participants screened, do I have to interview everyone, how is the recruitment team involved?).  Second Iteration should also firmly depict our association with GE Digital, which might require further discussion within GhostRed to determine our ideal direction in this area. Finally the content also needs to address unfulfilled motivations of personas, ex. Why go to GhostRed if I could just apply to a job? Why run a GhostRed event over a job fair?
