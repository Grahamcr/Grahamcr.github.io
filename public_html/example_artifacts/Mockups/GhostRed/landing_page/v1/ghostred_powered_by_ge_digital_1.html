Bring GhostRed to You

GhostRed was started as a volunteer effort at GE Digital's Information Security Technology Center (ISTC) by passionate GE engineers who wanted to create a fun way for high school students to get engaged with Capture the Flag competitions.  Since then it has to grown to be an official partnership with GE Digital, serving as one of its many out-reach and recruiting events.
