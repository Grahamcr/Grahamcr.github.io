The Challenge:

Every year millions of dollars in lost productivity is occurred by large industrial companies as they __X__.  Your challenge will be to work with stakeholders from a variety of business to understand what challenges they face and then work as a team to develop a complete solution to the ___X___ problem.  This will take a cross-functional team to accomplish - the more diverse your teams skills set the better. The team who has both the best solution plan and who has made the most meaningful progress toward that plan will be our champion of the Hackathon.
