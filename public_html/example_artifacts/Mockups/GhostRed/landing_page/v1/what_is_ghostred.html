The Industrial Take on Technology Competitions

GhostRed is the Industrial Focused Technology Events designed to drag you out of your comfort zone and tackle some of the most challenging digital problems facing the industrial world today.  Its about taking the skills you have, combined with those of your teammates and learning how to use them in a different way to solve challenges that require teamwork, cross-functional thinking, persistence, and imagination.
