A Big Search Bar with a Very Specific Purpose

We're Not Your Typical Search Engine

AnswerForge was built specifically for you.  We found that most of the time  Employees know exactly what we need in order to do our jobs, we just don't always know where to find it or who the right person is to contact about what we are trying to accomplish. Therefore we built an Answer Engine which thrives on specifics, unlike traditional search engines which struggle to give accurate results when given long or complex searches.

So What Does This All Mean?

It means we want you to tell us everything you possibly can about what you're looking for.  We're not kidding - tell us everything.  Just watch, the more you tell us, the more accurate we can be.

What's with the Different Colors?

Great Question! While you type our Context Zebra is busy at work picking out the important points of your search.  Once he's finished you can tell us more about that part of your question to get even better results by just clicking on a highlighted term.
