We're Presuing A Way To Find Anything

The FindIT Initiative is an IT Project from Innovation Studio which hopes to map everything and every relationship, from people to technology assests to skills and projects. How will we achieve such a feat? With the help of the most brilliant people we know - you.

Contextual

Answer Focused

Accurate

Community Driven

We're building something more than a database of things to scan through, we're buliding a digital network of relationshipts between people, places, skills & things.  This will give us the power to only return results relevant to you.

The goal of the FindIT Initiative isn't like that of a search engine.  Instead of giving you a list of possible links that might be helpful, we are interested in just returning the answer you need to start doing your work.

So much information at Ipusm doesn't actually exist in a digital form that can be found from a computer.  FindIT makes finding this information possible by involving you in the process of building our knowledge base.

Tired of outdated and inaccurate information? We are too.  That's why FindIT is a community driven project within Ipsum.  Being community driven means FindIT can receive the most up to date and accurate information possible.
