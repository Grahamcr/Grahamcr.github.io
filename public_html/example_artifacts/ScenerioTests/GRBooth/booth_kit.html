Register/Sign Up

• First-Time Participant creates a universal GR account on tablet and then registers to compete in GR Challenge


• Returning Participant signs in to compete again in the GR Challenge


• Both types (new and returning) are then added to the waiting participant queue

 

View Register/Signup System

Participant's Turn

• Participant Will See Their Name Appear on the Leaderboard and Receive A Text Notification If They Opted-In To Do So During The Registration Progress


•  To Start the Challenge, the Participant Will Hit the Start Button - Which kicks off the countdown and sounds the horn when it's time to start

 

View the Leaderboard

Stage One

• In this stage the participant will be asked a series of industrial-themed puzzles


•  To complete the stage the participant must answer two correctly or exceed 5 minutes.

•

• For each right answer the lights will flash and the bell will ding.  Incorrect answers get a want-wa... sound


• The leaderboard changes to show the participants progress

See Stage 1/2 Screens

View Correct Answer Interaction

View Incorrect Answer Interaction

Stage One - Complete

When the participant completes the first stage or runs out of time the booth will make the appropriate sound effect and the lights will flash


• The board will update to show the first stage time to complete


• The participant will be presented with the splash screen for the second stage

See Stage 1/2 Screens

Stage Two

• In this stage the participant will be asked a series of GE Trivia Questions


•  To complete the stage the participant must answer two correctly or exceed 5 minutes.


• For each right answer the lights will flash and the bell will ding.  Incorrect answers get a want-wa... sound


• As before the Leaderboard will show the participant's progress

See Stage 1/2 Screens

View Correct Answer Interaction

View Incorrect Answer Interaction

Stage Two - Complete

• When the participant completes (or runs out of time as shown here) the second stage the booth will make the appropriate sound effect


• The board will update to show the second stage time


• The participant will be directed to the third stage

See Stage 1/2 Screens

Stage 3

• In this stage the participant will need to use a lock pick set to open the box.  The participant will get a maxium of 5 minutes to complete this task


• Once the box is open the participant will see a button they can push to stop the timer and complete the challenge

Open the Box

Finished

• When the participant completes the challenge the "Victory" sound effect will go off and the lights will flash


• The leaderboard will update to show the stats the participant achieved throughout the challenge

View Leaderboard Screens
