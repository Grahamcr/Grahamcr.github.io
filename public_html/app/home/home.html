I am self-taught UX Architect who believes in using UX Practices to implement Design Thinking with a focus on making data-driven designs decisions.

Empathize

This is my chance to conduct research in order to develop knowledge about what your users do, say, think, and feel. What does this look like? It looks like taking time to directly observe what users do, what motivates them, what discourages them, what do you they need and what causes them frustration?
