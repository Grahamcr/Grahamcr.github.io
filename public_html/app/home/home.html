I guess you could call me a “Full Stack” UX Professional who loves to translate observations into well-thought-out experiments. These results enable me to create data-derived designs and define meaningful success metrics.

I am passionate about keeping the human in human-centered design and feel that nothing can replace in-person feedback and conversation around an idea, design or process. I am also enamored by the idea of using UX to solve problems bigger than software by applying the same techniques to physical problems.

Make Data Driven Decisions

What is the best way to end design arguments? Data! While it can be painful to watch all the unexpected ways users interact with your design it is a beautiful thing when a design gets to a point where users just "get it". I always find it interesting to dig into user's expectations for different elements of a design and work together with users to determine why they made the choices they did.
