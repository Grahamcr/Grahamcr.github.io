the space race of our genteration

Just about every automaker in the world is currently working on developing some form of a self driving vehicle. This is the story of how I helped one of those auto makers decrease their development time and ease saftey concerns.

To comply with my confidentiality agreement I have omitted and appropriated confidential information. Some designs are reinterpretations of the original

MY ROLE

I took over this project from another UX Researcher as the project just started to enter its second phase. I independently produced and tested all the major deliverables and presented to the project stakeholders. The research I conducted helped to define the project scope and direction.

CUSTOMER INSIGHTS & IDEATION

Through research I uncovered insights and translated concepts into features that addressed customer goals and motivations. The ideation sessions I conducted produced the feature set we prioritized as a team.

EXPERIENCE STRATEGY & VISION

I created prototypes to share the vision, design principles and content strategy. Through these artifacts and their presentation I helped to evangelize ideas and form consensus around a design direction

REQUIREMENTS GATHERING

I conducted research interviews with individuals representing a variety of domain skills and translated those interviews into user goals. From those goals I was able to derive functionality and advocate for the user in prioritization discussions.

PLANNING & DEVELOPMENT STRATEGY

I worked with the business owner and technical project manager to define our solution by balancing business goals and advocating for the user based on the research I had conducted.

TECHNICAL EXPERTISE

I leveraged my front-end development experience to create a style guide and enforce heuristic best practices.
